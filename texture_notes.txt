textures - 2d images (can be 3d or 1d)
tell each vertex of the triangle which part of the texture it corresponds to
each vertex then has a texture coordinate
texture coords - 0 to 1 (x and y axis)
sampling - retrieving texture color using texture coords

texture wrapping - when coordinates outside range (0-1)
repeat (def), mirrored repeat, clamp to edge (stretch edge), clamp to border (specific color)

glTexParameter*(GL_TEXTURE_2D, GL_TEXTURE_WRAP_S/T, GL_MIRRORED_REPEAT) s,t,r - x,y,z which texture axis

texel - texture pixel, for texture filtering
important when object large and texture small resolution
GL_NEAREST and GL_LINEAR (pixelated or smoother)
mipmaps - used when downscaling textures, same texture but twice smaller

glTexParameteri(GL_TEXTURE_2D, GL_TEXTURE_MIN_FILTER, GL_LINEAR_MIPMAP_LINEAR)
glTexParameteri(GL_TEXTURE_2D, GL_TEXTURE_MAG_FILTER, GL_LINEAR) - magnifying doesn't use mipmaps
